Projects

CODECA undertakes several projects with the aim to ensure social inclusion of marginalized people or at risk population. This projects run in collaboration with other public services and professional bodies in Cyprus, to which the organisation provides its scientific expertise and research capabilities in the areas of social development and cohesion.

Moreover, CODECA's projects include supervision for individuals and groups in the private as well as the public sector. Since 2019 CODECA has been granted projects on providing its expertise in the field of migration.
