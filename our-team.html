Our Team

CODECA is and continues to be powered by talented people who with their expertise and experience add value to our stakeholders. With their unique personality, each team member adopts CODECA’s mission, vision, and values.


CODECA's founding members have extensive expertise in the field of social policy and welfare at local, regional, and European level. Our team members continue to lead with their expertise and experience in the field offering our stakeholders personalized and unique experiences.


CODECA currently employs around 190 professionals including subsidiaries.
