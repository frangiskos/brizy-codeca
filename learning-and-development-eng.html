Learning & Development

CODECA fosters a learning-for-all culture in which people are encouraged and inspired to continue learning new skills. Our aim is to mobilise, inspire and promote lifelong learning, research, and continuously increase knowledge, skills, and competencies.

CODECA Learning HUB delivers training and supports our workforce and our EU projects.

We encourage our workforce to embrace lifelong learning by offering internal and external Training, Sponsorship to Academic and professional certifications, personal and professional growth.

 

CODECA Learning Beez delivers training and supports visiting interns locally and globally.
