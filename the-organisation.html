The Organisation

About us

CODECA is a non-profitable organisation established and operating in Cyprus since 2016. Our aim is to foster and promote social cohesion, development, and care.

To achieve this, we offer a number of services oriented towards social cohesion and welfare.

Read more 

Our Team

CODECA is and continues to be reinforced from talented people who offer to the organisation unique experiences and expertise. Each person contributes with its uniqueness and has the skills and knowledge to respond to the organisation's vision, mission and values.

Read more 

Child Protection Policy

CODECA within its course of action has proclaimed a 'Child Protection Policy' and guidelines to ensure the safety and protection of every child coming into contact with the organisation. The policy applies to all sectors of the organisation and to all personnel working for CODECA.

We Care About the Environment

Recognising the need to improve environmental performance based on the principles of sustainable development, in compliance with legislation and international standards, CODECA aims to achieve eco-friendly growth.
